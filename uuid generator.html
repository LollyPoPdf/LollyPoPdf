UUID Generator Tool

Generate unique identifiers instantly with our free UUID generator. Create UUIDs in various formats, copy them to clipboard, and download lists for your projects. All processing happens locally in your browser for complete privacy.

How to Use the UUID Generator

1

Configure Options

Select UUID version, number of UUIDs to generate, format, and other options. The tool supports UUID v1, v4, and v5 (simulated).

2

Generate UUIDs

Click the "Generate UUIDs" button to create unique identifiers instantly. Watch as they appear in the display area with options to copy individual UUIDs.

3

Copy or Download

Copy individual UUIDs to clipboard or download the entire list in your preferred format (TXT, JSON, CSV). All processing happens locally in your browser.

4

Manage History

View your UUID generation history, copy previous UUIDs, or clear the history. Your data stays in your browser and never leaves your device.
