Free Crop Images Instantly

Free online tool to crop your images to specific dimensions. Choose from preset aspect ratios or set custom dimensions. No registration required.

How to Crop your Images

1

Upload Your Image

Click the upload area or drag and drop your image file. We support JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP and more.

2

Select Aspect Ratio

Choose from preset aspect ratios like 16:9, 4:3, 1:1 or set custom dimensions for freeform cropping.

3

Adjust & Crop

Adjust the cropping area on your image, then click the crop button to process your image.

4

Download Result

Download your cropped image with the click of a button. No watermarks, no registration required.
