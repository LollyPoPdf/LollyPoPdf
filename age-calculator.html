Age Calculator Tool

Calculate your exact age in years, months, days, hours, minutes, and seconds. All processing happens locally with complete privacy.

How to Use the Age Calculator

1

Enter Your Birth Date

Select your date of birth using the date picker. You can also optionally select a different "current date" to calculate age at a specific point in time.

2

Enable Real-Time Updates

Toggle the real-time update option to see your age update every second with hours, minutes, and seconds.

3

View Detailed Results

See your age broken down into years, months, days, and more. Get interesting statistics and fun facts about your age.

4

Share or Clear

Share your age calculation results or clear all inputs to start fresh. Your data never leaves your browser.
