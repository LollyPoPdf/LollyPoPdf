Free Word Counter Tool

Count words, characters, sentences, paragraphs and more with our free tool. Supports text files, Word documents, and PDFs. Analyze your text in real-time with no registration required.

How to Use the Word Counter

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste your text into the input area. You can also upload text files, Word documents (.doc, .docx), or PDF files for analysis. The tool works with any amount of text.

2

View Real-Time Stats

Watch as the tool instantly calculates word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, and other statistics as you type or upload files.

3

Analyze Advanced Metrics

Explore advanced statistics like reading time, average word length, keyword density, and more detailed insights about your text.

4

Export & Save

Copy your text or download the statistics. Your work is automatically saved for your next session. All processing happens in your browser.
