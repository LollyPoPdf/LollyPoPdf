Universal Screen Recorder

Record your screen directly in your browser. Works on all devices including mobile phones, tablets, and PCs. No installation required. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the Screen Recorder

1

Configure Recording Settings

Choose your preferred video quality and audio settings. For mobile devices, follow the specific instructions provided.

2

Start Recording

Click the "Start Recording" button. If standard recording fails, use the alternative method for mobile devices.

3

Record Your Content

Record your screen, presentation, tutorial, or meeting. Watch the timer and recording indicator to track your progress.

4

Stop & Download

Click "Stop Recording" when finished. Preview your recording and download it in WebM or MP4 format with one click.
