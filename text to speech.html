Text to Speech Tool

Convert text to speech instantly using your browser's built-in speech synthesis. No downloads, no registration, and completely free. All processing happens locally in your browser.

How to Use the Text to Speech Tool

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste the text you want to convert to speech in the text area. There's no character limit, but very long texts may take longer to process.

2

Customize Settings

Select your preferred language, voice, and adjust the speech parameters like rate, pitch, and volume to your liking.

3

Listen to Speech

Click "Speak" to start the text-to-speech conversion. You can pause or resume the speech at any time using the control buttons.

4

Clear and Start Over

Use the "Clear" button to reset the text and settings, or simply enter new text to generate more speech.
