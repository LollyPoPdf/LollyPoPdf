Color Picker From Image Tool

Extract colors from any image with precision. Pick individual colors or generate complete palettes. All processing happens locally in your browser with complete privacy.

How to Use the Color Picker

1

Upload Your Image

Drag and drop or click to upload your image. The tool supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and other common formats with no file size limits.

2

Pick Colors

Click anywhere on the image to pick a color. Use the magnifier for pixel-perfect precision. The selected color will be displayed with its codes.

3

Generate Palette

Extract a complete color palette from your image. Choose the number of colors and let the tool generate harmonious palettes automatically.

4

Copy & Export

Copy color codes with one click or export your palette in various formats. All processing happens locally - your images never leave your device.
