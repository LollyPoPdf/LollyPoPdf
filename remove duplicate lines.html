Advanced Screen Recorder

Record your screen, webcam, and audio directly in your browser. No installation required. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the Screen Recorder

1

Configure Recording Settings

Choose what you want to record - screen, webcam, or both. Select audio sources and adjust quality settings for optimal recording.

2

Start Recording

Click the "Start Recording" button. Your browser will ask for permission to record your screen and/or audio. Grant permission to begin.

3

Record Your Content

Record your screen, presentation, tutorial, or meeting. Watch the timer and recording indicator to track your progress.

4

Stop & Download

Click "Stop Recording" when finished. Preview your recording and download it in WebM or MP4 format with one click.

Screen Recorder

Screen recording may not work on all browsers. For best results, use Chrome, Edge, or Firefox.
For system audio recording, use Chrome on Windows/macOS and enable "Share audio" when prompted. Microphone audio works on all browsers.
