BMI Calculator Tool

Calculate your Body Mass Index instantly with health category, ideal weight range, and personalized insights. All processing happens locally with complete privacy.

How to Use the BMI Calculator

1

Choose Your Unit System

Select between Metric (kg, cm) or Imperial (lbs, ft/in) units based on your preference. The calculator will automatically adjust the inputs.

2

Enter Your Measurements

Input your weight and height in the appropriate fields. For imperial units, you can enter feet and inches separately.

3

Calculate Your BMI

Click the "Calculate BMI" button to instantly see your BMI value, health category, and personalized insights.

4

Understand Your Results

Review your BMI category, ideal weight range, and health tips. Track your progress with the history feature.
