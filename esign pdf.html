eSign PDF Tool

Sign PDF documents electronically with precision. Draw your signature directly on the PDF with customizable color, thickness, and size. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use eSign PDF Tool

1

Upload PDF

Upload the PDF document you want to sign by dragging & dropping or browsing your files.

2

Select Area

Click and drag on the PDF to select the exact location where you want to place your signature.

3

Draw Signature

Use your mouse or touchpad to draw your signature directly in the selected area.

4

Download

Download your signed PDF document with the signature placed exactly where you selected.
