Audio Converter Tool

Convert audio files between formats, trim, adjust volume, and more. Process large files with no limits. All processing happens in your browser with 100% privacy.

How to Use the Audio Converter

1

Upload Audio File

Drag and drop or click to upload your audio file. The tool supports MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, AAC, M4A, and more. No file size limits.

2

Choose Conversion Options

Select output format, bitrate, sample rate, and other options. You can also trim the audio or adjust volume before conversion.

3

Process & Preview

Click "Convert Audio" to process your file. View the waveform preview and listen to the converted audio before downloading.

4

Download Result

Download your converted audio file or convert another file. All processing happens locally in your browser with no server uploads.
