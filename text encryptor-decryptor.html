Text Encryptor/Decryptor Tool

Secure your sensitive data with military-grade encryption. Encrypt and decrypt text using AES-256, Base64, or Caesar cipher. All processing happens locally in your browser with no data sent to servers.

How to Use the Text Encryptor/Decryptor

1

Enter Your Text

Type, paste, or upload your text to encrypt/decrypt. The tool supports text files and any amount of text with no limitations.

2

Set Password & Method

Choose a strong password and select encryption method. AES-256 is recommended for maximum security.

3

Process & Review

Click "Encrypt" or "Decrypt" to process your text. View the security indicators and review the output in the right panel.

4

Export Results

Copy the encrypted/decrypted text to clipboard or export it as a file. All processing happens locally in your browser.
