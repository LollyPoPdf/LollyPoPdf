Regex Tester Tool

Test your regular expressions in real-time with full match highlighting, groups, and error detection. All processing happens locally with complete privacy.

How to Use the Regex Tester

1

Enter Your Regex

Type your regular expression pattern in the input field. Use flags like 'g' (global), 'i' (case-insensitive), 'm' (multiline), etc. to modify matching behavior.

2

Provide Test Text

Enter or paste the text you want to test your regex against. You'll see real-time highlighting of matches as you type.

3

Analyze Results

View detailed match information including positions, groups, and full match values. All processing happens instantly in your browser.

4

Copy or Clear

Copy your results to clipboard or clear all inputs to start fresh. Your data never leaves your browser.
