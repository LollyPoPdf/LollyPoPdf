Text to Speech Tool

Convert text to natural-sounding speech with multiple voices and languages. Customize speed and pitch, and enjoy unlimited usage - all client-side with complete privacy.

How to Use the Text to Speech Tool

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste your text into the input box. You can also upload a text file or use the copy button to copy text to clipboard.

2

Select Voice & Language

Choose from multiple voices (male/female) and languages. Preview voices before generating the full audio.

3

Customize Settings

Adjust speech rate, pitch, and volume to your preference.

4

Generate Audio

Click 'Speak' to generate the audio. Listen to the result using the play, pause and stop controls.

Text to Speech
