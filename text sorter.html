Text Sorter Tool

Quickly sort your text lines alphabetically, numerically, or by length. Process large files, customize options, and export results. All processing happens in your browser with no limits.

How to Use the Text Sorter

1

Enter Your Text

Type, paste, or upload your text containing lines to sort. The tool supports text files, Word documents, and PDFs. You can process any amount of text with no limitations.

2

Configure Options

Customize how your text is sorted. Choose from alphabetical, numerical, natural sorting, or sort by line length. Configure case sensitivity, trimming, and more.

3

Process & Review

Click "Sort Text" to process your text. View the statistics showing how many lines were sorted and review the organized output in the right panel.

4

Export Results

Copy the sorted text to clipboard or export it as a text file. You can also reverse the order or shuffle lines randomly. All processing happens locally in your browser.
