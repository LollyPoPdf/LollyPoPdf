Email Generator Tool

Generate temporary,disposable email addresses instantly.Perfect for testing,sign-ups,and protecting your privacy.All processing happens locally with complete anonymity.

How to Use the Email Generator

1

Generate Email

Click the"Generate Email"button to create a new temporary email address.Customize the username and domain if needed.

2

Copy&Use

Copy your generated email address and use it for sign-ups,testing,or any situation where you need a temporary inbox.

3

Check Inbox

Monitor your inbox for incoming messages.Emails will appear automatically when they arrive at your temporary address.

4

Stay Private

Your temporary email expires automatically.All data is stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server.

Voice Command Control

Control the email generator hands-free using voice commands.Click the microphone button to start listening.The system supports natural language and number recognition.

You can say numbers like"five","20",or"one hundred".Voice control works continuously after initial permission and can be stopped/restarted anytime without refreshing.

Click microphone to start voice control

Basic Commands

"Generate email"-Create new email address
"Copy email"-Copy current email to clipboard
"Generate[5-100]emails"-Create multiple emails(5 to 100)
"Generate[5-100]Gmail"-Create multiple Gmail accounts

Advanced Commands

"Refresh inbox"-Check for new emails
"Clear all"-Reset everything
"Clear all gmail"-Clear all Gmail IDs
"Dark mode"/"Light mode"-Toggle theme
"Help"-Show available commands
"Stop listening"-Turn off voice control
