QR Code Generator & Scanner Tool

Create and scan QR codes instantly - generate custom QR codes for URLs, text, contacts and more. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the QR Code Tool

1

Generate QR Codes

Enter text, URL, or contact information and customize the QR code with colors, size, and error correction level. Preview your QR code in real-time.

2

Scan QR Codes

Use your device camera to scan QR codes in real-time or upload an image containing a QR code. The tool will decode the content instantly.

3

Customize & Download

Adjust QR code appearance with custom colors, add logos, and choose the perfect size. Download your QR code as PNG or SVG.

4

Save & Manage

Access your generated and scanned QR codes from the history section. All data is stored locally in your browser for privacy.
