GST Calculator Tool

Calculate GST amounts instantly for any product or service. Supports both GST-inclusive and GST-exclusive calculations with detailed breakdowns and visual charts.

How to Use the GST Calculator

1

Enter Amount

Input the amount you want to calculate GST for. This can be either the GST-inclusive price or the GST-exclusive price.

2

Select GST Rate

Choose the applicable GST rate from the predefined options (5%, 12%, 18%, 28%) or enter a custom rate if needed.

3

Choose Calculation Type

Select whether your amount includes GST (GST-inclusive) or excludes GST (GST-exclusive) to get accurate calculations.

4

View Results

See the detailed breakdown of GST amount, net price, and total amount with a visual chart showing the tax distribution.
