Text to Speech Tool

Convert any text to natural-sounding speech with multiple voices and languages. All processing happens locally in your browser with no limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the Text to Speech Tool

1

Enter Your Text

Type or paste your text into the input field. You can also upload a text file. The tool supports all languages and unlimited text length.

2

Choose Language & Voice

Select your preferred language and voice from the available options. Preview voices before using them.

3

Adjust Audio Settings

Customize speech rate, pitch, and volume to your preference. Adjust settings in real-time while the speech is playing.

4

Listen to Speech

Click the speak button to convert your text to speech. All processing happens locally - your text never leaves your device.
