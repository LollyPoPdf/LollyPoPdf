Color Blindness Simulator Tool

See how your images appear to people with different types of color vision deficiencies. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the Color Blindness Simulator

1

Upload Your Image

Drag and drop or click to upload your image file. The tool supports JPG, PNG, GIF, and WEBP formats with no file size limits.

2

Select Color Blindness Type

Choose from various types of color vision deficiencies including Protanopia, Deuteranopia, Tritanopia, and Achromatopsia.

3

Simulate & Preview

Click "Simulate" to process the image. Compare the original and simulated versions side by side.

4

Download Result

Download your simulated image with one click. All processing happens locally in your browser - your files never leave your device.
