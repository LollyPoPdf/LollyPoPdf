Loan EMI Calculator

Calculate your Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and more. Plan your finances with accurate EMI calculations and detailed breakdowns.

How to Use the EMI Calculator

1

Enter Loan Details

Input the loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure. Use the sliders or type directly into the input fields.

2

Calculate EMI

Click the "Calculate EMI" button to instantly see your monthly payment amount and total repayment details.

3

Analyze Results

Review the detailed breakdown including total interest payable and see the payment distribution in the chart.

4

Plan Your Finances

Use the amortization schedule to understand how your payments are allocated between principal and interest over time.
