Audio Trimmer Tool

Trim audio files with precision - cut, trim, and edit your audio files directly in your browser. All processing happens locally with no file size limits and complete privacy.

How to Use the Audio Trimmer

1

Upload Your Audio

Drag and drop or click to upload your audio file. The tool supports MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG, AAC, M4A, MP4, and AMR formats with no file size limits.

2

Set Trim Points

Use the interactive waveform to select start and end points, or manually enter the times. Preview the original audio on the left.

3

Trim & Preview

Click "Trim Audio" to start processing. Watch the progress bar and preview the trimmed audio on the right when finished.

4

Download Result

Download your trimmed audio with one click. All processing happens locally in your browser - your files never leave your device.
