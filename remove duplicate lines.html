Remove Duplicate Lines Tool

Quickly remove duplicate lines from your text while preserving the original order. Process large files, customize options, and export results. All processing happens in your browser with no limits.

How to Use the Duplicate Line Remover

1

Enter Your Text

Type, paste, or upload your text containing duplicate lines. The tool supports text files, Word documents, and PDFs. You can process any amount of text with no limitations.

2

Configure Options

Customize how duplicates are detected. Choose to ignore case, ignore blank lines, sort before removing duplicates, or apply advanced filters to fine-tune the results.

3

Process & Review

Click "Remove Duplicates" to process your text. View the statistics showing how many duplicates were removed and review the clean output in the right panel.

4

Export Results

Copy the cleaned text to clipboard or export it as a text file. You can also export just the duplicate lines for review. All processing happens locally in your browser.
