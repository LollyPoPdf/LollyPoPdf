Currency Converter Tool

Convert between any currencies with live exchange rates. All processing happens locally with real-time data and complete privacy.

How to Use the Currency Converter

1

Select Currencies

Choose the currency you want to convert from and the currency you want to convert to from the dropdown menus.

2

Enter Amount

Type the amount you want to convert in the input field. The conversion happens automatically as you type.

3

View Results

See the converted amount instantly. You can also view historical charts and save favorite currency pairs.

4

Save & Share

Save your favorite currency pairs for quick access. All data is stored locally in your browser.

Voice Commands

Control everything hands-free with voice commands. Works on all devices.

Real-time Processing

Commands processed instantly as you speak. No delays.

Continuous Listening

Auto-restarts listening. Works seamlessly without interruption.

Cross-platform

Works perfectly on PC, Mobile, and Tablet devices.

Available Voice Commands

Say "Convert" to perform conversion
Say "Swap currencies" to switch currencies
Say "Set amount 100" to change amount
Say "From USD to EUR" to change currencies
Say "Add to favorites" to save pair
Say "Clear favorites" to clear favorite pairs
Say "Clear history" to clear conversions
Say "Dark mode" or "Light mode"
Say "Show chart 30" to change chart view
Say "Stop listening" to turn off voice
